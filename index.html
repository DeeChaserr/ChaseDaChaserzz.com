The (CDC) official faction.

CDC stands for Chase Da Chaserzz. It consists of players from various hood games on Roblox, such as Philly Streetz 2, South London 2, Street Life Remastered 2, and many more. We are a friendly and fun faction to play with, but we can also be competitive at times. We’re looking for more players to join and help grow the presence of CDC in Roblox hood games. Feel free to join, take part in wars against other factions, and work your way up the ranks as you play and grind.

CDC Rules:

CDC Rules Respect Members No toxicity, hate speech, or disrespect toward other members. Treat everyone as equals, regardless of rank. No Cheating/Exploiting Exploits, hacks, or cheats are strictly forbidden. If caught, you will be removed immediately. Stay Active Try to be active in CDC events, wars, and activities. If you need a break, let a leader know. Follow Chain of Command Listen to higher ranks during wars, raids, or training. Don’t argue with leaders in the middle of an event. No Drama Keep personal beef or issues outside of CDC. Don’t start unnecessary conflicts with allies or other factions. Represent CDC Well When wearing the CDC tag, act respectfully in public games. No trolling or giving the group a bad image. Grind & Improve Always look to improve your skills. Be open to learning and ranking up. Have Fun & Compete Remember, CDC is about playing together, having fun, and competing fairly.
